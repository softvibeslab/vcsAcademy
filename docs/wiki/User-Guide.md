## Training System

### 4-Stage Progression

**Stage 1: New Rep** (150 points)
- Duration: 1-2 weeks
- Focus: Build foundation
- Content: 15 modules

**Stage 2: Developing Rep** (300 points)
- Duration: 2-4 weeks
- Focus: Execute consistently
- Content: 15 modules

**Stage 3: Performing Rep** (500 points)
- Duration: 4-8 weeks
- Focus: Close consistently
- Content: 15 modules

**Stage 4: Top Producer** (750 points)
- Duration: 8-12 weeks
- Focus: Elite performance
- Content: Ongoing mastery

### 6 Training Tracks

1. **Pro Mindset** - Build the champion mindset
2. **Discovery & Control** - Master the sales process
3. **Value Architecture** - Present value effectively
4. **Decision Management** - Guide decisions confidently
5. **Objection Mastery** - Handle any objection
6. **Post-Decision Integrity** - Build long-term success

### Training Modules

Each module includes:
- **Video Lesson** (5-10 minutes)
- **Key Move** - One actionable takeaway
- **Action Items** - Practical application
- **Quiz** - Test understanding

**Tips for Module Completion:**
- Watch videos in a quiet environment
- Take notes on the Key Move
- Complete action items before moving on
- Apply what you learn on the floor

## Progress Tracking

### Readiness Score

Your readiness score combines:
- Video Completion (40%)
- Track Progress (30%)
- Quick Wins Applied (10%)
- Breakdowns Reviewed (10%)
- Training Streak (10%)

**Score Ranges:**
- 0-25: Getting Started
- 26-50: Building Foundation
- 51-75: Developing Skills
- 76-100: Ready to Excel

### Training Streak

Maintain consecutive days of training to build momentum:
- 24-hour window to count consecutive days
- Longer streaks = bonus points
- Streak freezes available for premium members

### Badges & Achievements

Earn badges for accomplishments:
- **First Steps** - Complete first module
- **Quick Learner** - Complete 5 modules in a week
- **Streak Master** - 7-day training streak
- **Knowledge Seeker** - Complete all tracks
- And more...

## Tips for Success

### Daily Routine

**Morning (15 minutes):**
1. Check your dashboard
2. Watch one quick win
3. Review yesterday's application

**Pre-Tour (5 minutes):**
1. Use Pre-Tour Mode
2. Review relevant objections
3. Focus your mindset

**Evening (10 minutes):**
1. Complete one module
2. Apply Key Move
3. Update progress

### Best Practices

**Consistency Over Intensity:**
- Train every day, even for 10 minutes
- Maintain your training streak
- Small progress adds up

**Apply Immediately:**
- Use Key Moves on the floor
- Practice new techniques
- Share results with community

**Engage with Community:**
- Ask questions
- Share experiences
- Help others succeed

**Track Your Progress:**
- Review your readiness score
- Celebrate achievements
- Set weekly goals

### Getting the Most Out of Training

**Active Learning:**
- Take notes on Key Moves
- Pause and reflect on concepts
- Plan how to apply each lesson

**On-the-Floor Application:**
- Try one new technique per tour
- Adapt approaches to your style
- Track what works best

**Continuous Improvement:**
- Review completed modules regularly
- Re-watch breakdowns for insights
- Stay updated with new content

## Troubleshooting

- Use search function
- Check different tracks
- Look in your Watch Later
